About my work

I am currently working for the CRISP (Cluster of Research Infrastructures for Synergies in Physics) project. The objetives of my new project are to develop and deploy a pan-European system for unique identification (Authentication and authorisation infrastructure: AAI) of users at the infrastructures of the participating RIs EuroFEL (PSI), ESRF, ESS, FAIR (GSI), ILL, and XFEL for the management of local and remote access to facilities, experiments, data, and IT resources.

About me

I graduated in Computing Engineer at the University of Sevilla (Spain) in 2007, and afterwards I got my MsC. in Grid Computing in Cranfield University in 2008. Right after I started to work in temporary projects. In 2009 I started at GSI in the Scientific Computing Group leaded by Peter Malzacher. My current project is funded by the CRISP project, which is inside the ESFRI roadmap.
